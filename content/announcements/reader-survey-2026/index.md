## What we ask

The survey covers three areas:

1. **Content**: Which sections do you use most? Which articles have been useful? What topics are you not seeing covered?
2. **Format**: How do you read Academis Demo — desktop, mobile, RSS? Is the current length and frequency right?
3. **Editorial priorities**: From a list of themes we are considering for the next year, which ones matter most to you?

## Why we do this

We are not a large publication with analytics infrastructure. We do not track individual readers. The survey is one of the few systematic ways we have of understanding whether the publication is useful to the people it is meant to be useful to.

Previous surveys have led to direct editorial changes: the *Cities, Data, and the Public Interest* series was launched partly in response to reader requests for more material on urban infrastructure and governance.
