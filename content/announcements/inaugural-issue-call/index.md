This was the founding call that shaped the first issue of Academis Demo. We circulated it through academic networks, several editorial mailing lists, and a handful of Mastodon accounts in November 2025.

We received thirty-four pitches over six weeks. Eight were commissioned; four were published in the inaugural issue and four are still in editing. The response confirmed a clear appetite for long-form analytical writing that is neither journalism nor peer-reviewed research.

Submissions are now permanently closed for this call. Future calls will be announced separately.
