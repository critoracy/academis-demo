This workshop ran from March to May 2026 across six sessions. Each session took one paper from a different discipline and worked through it slowly: argument structure, evidence quality, rhetorical choices, and the gap between abstract and conclusion.

Sessions were recorded. Notes from each session are available in the wiki.

The workshop is now closed. A second series is under consideration for autumn 2026.
