Finished coding the 1990–2024 dataset. One thing that keeps jumping out: countries that adopted mixed-member systems early have significantly lower electoral volatility scores than those that went proportional from the start.

Need to check whether this survives controlling for party system age — the two variables are correlated enough that I am not confident the effect is real rather than a proxy. Running the robustness checks this week.

The dataset will be published openly when the paper is submitted. If you want early access for replication or comparative work, get in touch.
