About

Historian and essayist based in Western Europe. I write on institutional history, the politics of evidence, and the long shadow of early-modern information technologies. I am completing a book on the epistemic consequences of the printing press in sixteenth-century Europe.

At Academis Demo I serve as editor-in-chief. My editorial policy is simple: rigour over reach, argument over assertion.
