Contributor Guide Updated — Version 2

The contributor guide has been substantially revised to clarify pitch format requirements, our review timeline, and what editors look for in a strong argument pitch.

The most important change: we now require pitches to state the central claim in one sentence before explaining the evidence base. This was the most common reason for rejection under the old process.
